Interface for creating, modifying, and viewing landmarks for the currently selected account.

The location of a landmark can be specified in three different ways:

  1. By manually entering the Latitude and Longitude values.
  2. By clicking on the desired map location, which will populate the Latitude and Longitude fields.
  3. By entering the location address into the Address box before clicking on the Preview button to populate the Latitude and Longitude fields.

Changes to Fence Radius and Color can be visualized py clicking the Preview button.

Landmark Name must be unique within the account.

Click the Save button to persist a new landmark or changes to an existing one.

Note that saving an existing landmark after changing only the Name creates a new landmark at the exact same location. This feature may be used for creating concentric landmarks with different fence radii.

To create overlapping but offset landmarks, it may be necessary to first reduce the radii of existing landmarks that would be overlapped. Then after creating the new landmark restore the affected fence offsets to their desired values.
