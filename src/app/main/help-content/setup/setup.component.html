The setup interfaces are used for managing the application and its accounts.

Users with Principal View access can, within the scope of their assigned user roles, view and manage the application accounts, users, and devices. Permitted users obtain this view by selecting Principal on the main menu bar account drop-down menu.

Users with Account View access can, also within the scope of their assigned user roles, view and manage resources within the accounts to which they belong.
