Lists the notifications being subscribed to for the current account.

A notification subscription will, when the set conditions are met, cause an entry to be made in the NOTIFICATIONS list. In addition to this list, Notification Subscribers can be created for sending email and text messages to registered account users.

Notification subscriptions having been assigned multiple devices will have a more (...) symbol in their Devices field. Click to the right of the symbol to open up the list of devices.

Double-click on a subscription row to view and edit that subscription.

Currently, notifications can be issued for landmark arrivals and departures.
